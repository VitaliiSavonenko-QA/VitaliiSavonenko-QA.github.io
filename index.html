How I approach testing

I combine structured test coverage with exploratory thinking. For games, I pay attention to both technical correctness and how the experience feels to a player.

1

Understand the feature, user flow, and risks before testing.

2

Create practical coverage: smoke, regression, edge cases, negative scenarios.

3

Investigate issues through UI, API, logs, analytics, and reproducible steps.

4

Report bugs clearly and explain impact, not just symptoms.
